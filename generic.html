My Most Noteworthy Projects

Olive

Olive is an open source project designed with nonprofits in mind. It essentially works as a template application and can be used by any nonprofit that tracks clients and client intake.

The only downside is that each business needs to deploy their own version of it on the web. I do, however, cover how to do this in the documentation.

Check it out

Cricket's Adventure

Cricket's adventure was one of my first big projects. I did it as a collaborative project where I had one other person do all the art, and I coded everything in Pygame. It's a 2-D Platformer where you play as a cat trying to get into a box while projectiles are flying at you.

The art style was never optimized but it runs great after testing on a few different computers.
